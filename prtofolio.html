about me

My name is Rein, and like most teens, I'm navigating the exciting and sometimes overwhelming journey of figuring out who I am and what I love. I am driven by a passion for self-expression and connection, always looking for ways to blend my creative interests with the academic path I'm on. I enjoy spending time with my friends, but I also value my solo time for recharging and focusing on my personal projects.

hobby

My absolute favorite hobby is Digital Art and Photography. I love using my phone and editing software to capture the world around me and transform ordinary images into unique, colorful pieces of work. This hobby allows me to express my feelings, practice composition and design, and share my unique perspective on social media, which is a great way to get feedback and connect with other creative people my age.
